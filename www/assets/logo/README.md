# Logo Assets

Runtime logo assets for the static frontend.

- `radar-logo-dark.png` and `radar-logo-light.png` are the full-size cropped
  source PNGs.
- `radar-logo-*-512.png` and `radar-logo-*-192.png` are app/icon scale assets.
- `radar-logo-*-96.png` is used by the topbar brand mark.
- `radar-logo-*-32.png` and `radar-logo-*-16.png` are favicon-scale assets.

The light and dark marks intentionally differ by theme: a lighter radar
treatment on light surfaces and a black radar screen treatment on dark
surfaces.
